Our Purpose

From the Stage
to the Front Door

America's veterans sacrificed everything. Too many come home to uncertainty, instability, and streets instead of shelter. Phoenixx Rising Ventures uses the raw, unrelenting power of live rock music to change that — one show, one city, one veteran at a time.

  • 🏠
    Fund Veteran Housing 100% of tour proceeds support stable housing through the Veterans Community Project.
  • 🎸
    Rock Music as a Force for Good Sonic Octane delivers electrifying performances that unite communities around the cause.
  • 📡
    285M+ Household Reach Every show broadcast across national TV, radio, and social platforms.
