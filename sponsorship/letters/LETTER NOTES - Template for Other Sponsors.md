# Sponsorship Letter — Template (All Other Prospects)
**Status: DRAFT — Placeholders need to be filled before sending**

## Who This Goes To
Businesses, celebrities, professional athletes, politicians, and other sponsor prospects.

## Placeholders to Replace Before Sending
- `[Business Name]` → the company or organization name
- `[Contact Name, Title]` → full name and job title of the contact
- `[Contact First Name]` → first name only (used in greeting)

## How to Use This Template
1. Open the PDF
2. Copy the text into a Word doc or Google Doc
3. Replace all [Placeholder] fields with the specific person's info
4. Personalize 1-2 lines about why their brand fits the tour
5. Save as new PDF and send
